Explorer Account

What's an "Explorer" Account?

Every great expedition begins at basecamp. Think of Explorer as your passport to the frontier—perfect if you’re just curious about pickleball or gearing up for your first adventure. Stay as long as you need, and when you’re ready to hit the courts more often, you can upgrade to a membership at anytime.

What you can do with an Explorer account

  • Book a court for you and your fellow Explorers
  • Purchase an Explorer PassAll day Open Play pass
  • Sign waivers once and go
  • RSVP for events & clinics and get schedule updates
  • Manage your profile & payments in one place

Free to create. You only pay for what you play.

Already a member?

If you’ve purchased a Trailblazer or Pioneer membership, you’re all set—no need to create an Explorer account. Your membership includes account access and booking privileges out of the box.
